About

Sun, Jan 1, 2017

This is a personal blog where I might occasionally post about things that interest me, mostly coffee-related.

I enjoy thinking, talking and writing about coffee and messing about with data. I was previously an Economist at the International Coffee Organization, working on market analysis and statistics.

I can be found on Twitter and also LinkedIn.

I am also available for research and consultancy work.

This blog is built on Hugo using the blogdown R package, and the theme is Hyde.
